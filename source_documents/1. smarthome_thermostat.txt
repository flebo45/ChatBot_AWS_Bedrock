SmartHome Thermostat User Manual

Reset Instructions:
To reset your SmartHome thermostat, press and hold the reset button for 10 seconds until the display flashes. This will restore the device to its default factory settings.

Temperature Settings:
You can set the desired temperature using the up and down arrows on the main screen. The thermostat supports a range from 10°C to 30°C.

Wi-Fi Setup:
Go to the Settings menu, select Wi-Fi, and choose your network. Enter your password when prompted.
