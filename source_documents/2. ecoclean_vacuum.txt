EcoClean Vacuum Cleaner Product Guide

Warranty Information:
The EcoClean vacuum cleaner includes a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase. The warranty covers manufacturer defects and excludes filters and physical damage.

Filter Maintenance:
For optimal performance, clean the HEPA filter every 30 days and replace it every 6 months.

Storage:
Store in a cool, dry place. Avoid wrapping the power cord tightly around the unit to prevent damage.
