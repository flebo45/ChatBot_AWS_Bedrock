SoundMax Bluetooth Speaker Setup

Bluetooth Pairing:
1. Press the Bluetooth icon until the LED begins flashing.
2. Open your phone’s Bluetooth settings.
3. Select “SoundMax” from the list of available devices.

Charging:
Use the included USB-C cable. A red light indicates charging; it turns green when fully charged.

Battery Life:
Up to 10 hours on a full charge with medium volume.
