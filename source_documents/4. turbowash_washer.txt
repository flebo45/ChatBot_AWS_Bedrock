TurboWash Washing Machine User Manual

Load Capacity:
This model supports up to 8 kg of laundry per cycle. Overloading the machine may cause performance issues.

Wash Cycles:
Includes standard, quick, delicate, and heavy-duty cycles. Use cold water for synthetic fabrics and warm water for cotton.

Cleaning the Drum:
Run a drum-clean cycle monthly using a descaler tablet to prevent buildup.
